Five Key Findings from the Analysis¶

The findings from the above analysis includes:

1.The biggest sales of the year is from Spain,the company should look for ways to invest more in the country,if possible the company should reduce the cost of advertising and invest it in the country like Mexico where there are low sales

2.The number of children does not affect or have any influence on the sales as the highest sales comes from Family with no children.

3.The company need to direct it advertising effort to other countries as over 70 percent of the sales come from one out of the eight countries where the company operates.

4.The least sales come from the family with the highest number of children,which means the company product does not appeal enough to large family as the relationship shows the higher the number of children per family the lower the sales.

5.The product with highest sales for the year is Wine.
